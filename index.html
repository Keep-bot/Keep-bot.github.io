This is the web end of Keep.

Keep is a simple platform to send data (text and files) from this web page directly to your telegram account, to the bot end.

This can be used to keep (save) your files on telegram even when you have no access to your telegram.

The bot end of Keep is @allkeepbot.

How to use keep?
1 Start our telegram Bot @allkeepbot;
2 You get your User Id, when you start the bot;
3 Enter your User Id and the data you want to save;
4 The data will be sent to your account via our bot.
